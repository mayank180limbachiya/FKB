Belt tension Calculation based on Acoustic or Optical type meters


About Belt tension:

The vibration frequency of a plucked string is dependent upon the tension of that string. As the tension is increased, the vibration frequency also increases.here is a direct relationship between belt tension and a belt’s natural frequency of vibration. This relationship holds true except for the very extreme high-tension zones (well above where any belt system can operate). For belt spans greater than 0.25m, this will provide results within 10% of the actual values.
