WN69 — I/O Link DO Alarm

CH(n) Gxx Syy zz : PMCm Ybbb = **H

About this Alarm

DO alarm at byte zz of slot yy of group xx in channel n, Y address bbb of PMC path m. The **H value shows alarm bits in hexadecimal. Refer to FANUC 0i-F CONNECTION MANUAL (B-64603EN) for system relay area details.

Alarm Input

WN69 Alarm CH( G S :PMC Y = H)
n=Channel xx=Group yy zz=Slot/Byte m=PMC Path bbb=Y Addr **H=Hex bits
